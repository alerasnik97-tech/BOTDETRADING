# NEXT TRAIN-ONLY PROTOCOL BO01/MR02 DESIGN V1

## 1. Protocol Definition and Name
- **Suggested Protocol Name:** `M2_TRAIN_ONLY_LIMITED_STRUCTURAL_EVALUATION_PROTOCOL`
- **Objective:** Transition from M1 basic plumbing verification (3-day slice) to a pre-declared, non-optimized structural evaluation on real-market data to analyze strategy behavior, call safety, and signal frequency.
- **Strict Bounds:** **DESIGN-ONLY.** No code execution, data loading, backtesting, or parameter sweeps are authorized by this document.

---

## 2. Core Differences from M1
Unlike M1, which verified minimum plumbing on a 3-day window:
- M2 evaluates structural properties over a wider, pre-declared train-only window.
- **M2 DOES NOT evaluate performance or backtest metrics.** It does not select a "champion" or verify strategy profitability.
- It answers structural questions:
  1. Do `BO01` and `MR02` run without crashing on wider time-series data?
  2. Is the call frequency statistically meaningful for further research?
  3. Are there extreme temporal concentrations (hourly, monthly, or by trading session)?
  4. Are the fail-closed gates fully robust against unexpected column dropping over longer periods?
  5. Are there lookahead, cadence, or datetime alignment anomalies?

---

## 3. Data Policy
- **Asset Class:** EURUSD exclusively.
- **Timeframe:** M5 bars.
- **Data Source Path:** `05_MARKET_DATA_VAULT/eurusd_data/prepared_train_2015_2024/prepared/EURUSD_M5.csv` (read-only).
- **Rango Permitido:** `2015-01-01` to `2024-12-31` (Train-only partition).
- **Strict Exclusions:** No validation data, no holdout data, no 2025/2026 dates, and no result-based slice selection.

### Evaluation Options
- **Option A (Conservative M2):** 3-month continuous train-only window (`2015-01-01` to `2015-03-31`).
- **Option B (Broader M2):** 12-month continuous train-only window (`2015-01-01` to `2015-12-31`).
- **Audit Recommendation:** **Option A (Conservative M2)** is strongly recommended. It is optimal for initial operational stability and contract verification before performing wider-scale data ingestion.

---

## 4. Metrics Policy
To prevent any backtest leakage, overfitting, or self-deception, the allowed and prohibited metrics are strictly codified:

### Allowed Structural Metrics
- `row_count` (total candles processed)
- `signal_call_count` (total signal endpoint invocations)
- `valid_signal_count` (total buy/sell entries returned)
- `none_count` (total candles returning no action)
- `exception_count` (total caught crashes/exceptions)
- `days_with_signal` (count of calendar days having at least one signal)
- `max_signals_per_day` (peak session activity)
- `signals_by_hour` (hourly frequency distribution)
- `signals_by_month` (seasonal frequency distribution)
- `fail_closed_gate_checks` (contract failures under dropped columns)
- `forbidden_date_violations` (0 expected)
- `validation_holdout_access_attempts` (0 expected)

### Prohibited Performance Metrics
- **NO PnL (Profit and Loss)**
- **NO Profit Factor (PF)**
- **NO Win Rate (WR)**
- **NO Max Drawdown (DD) or Equity Curves**
- **NO Sharpe, Sortino, or Expectancy Ratios**
- **NO R-multiples or Profit per Trade**
- **NO Optimization score or Strategy ranking by profitability**
